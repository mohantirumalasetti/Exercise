Six-Month Exercise Plan

Month Strength Training Cardiovascular Exercise Abdominal Exercises weeks
Month 1

Strength Training

Exercise Sets Reps
Plank 3 Hold for 30 seconds each
Crunches 3 12 reps
Bicycle Crunches 3 10 reps on each side
Russian Twists 3 10 reps on each side

Cardiovascular Exercise

30 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio exercises such as jogging, cycling, or swimming.

Abdominal Exercises

Perform exercises like plank, crunches, bicycle crunches, and Russian twists.
Month 2

Strength Training

Exercise Sets Reps
Side Plank 3 Hold for 30 seconds on each side
Reverse Crunches 3 12 reps
Leg Raises 3 10 reps
Mountain Climbers 3 10 reps on each side

Cardiovascular Exercise

30 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio exercises such as running, elliptical training, or rowing.

Abdominal Exercises

Continue with abdominal exercises like plank variations, leg raises, and seated Russian twists.
Month 3

Strength Training

Exercise Sets Reps
Weighted Plank 3 Hold for 45 seconds each
Crunches with Medicine Ball 3 12 reps
Toe Touches 3 10 reps
Oblique Twists 3 10 reps on each side

Cardiovascular Exercise

45 minutes of moderate-intensity or high-intensity cardio exercises such as HIIT workouts, cycling sprints, or stair climbing.

Abdominal Exercises

Incorporate more challenging exercises like hanging leg raises, reverse crunches, and side plank variations.
Month 4

Strength Training

Exercise Sets Reps
Plank with Knee Tucks 3 Hold for 45 seconds each
Flutter Kicks 3 12 reps on each leg
Vertical Leg Crunches 3 10 reps
Woodchoppers 3 10 reps on each side

Cardiovascular Exercise

60 minutes of varied cardio exercises such as running, swimming, jumping rope, or HIIT workouts.

Abdominal Exercises

Continue with advanced exercises like hanging windshield wipers, dragon flags, and weighted crunches.
Month 5

Strength Training

Exercise Sets Reps
Side Plank with Leg Lifts 3 Hold for 45 seconds on each side
Scissor Kicks 3 12 reps on each leg
Plank Twists 3 10 reps on each side
Weighted Russian Twists 3 10 reps on each side

Cardiovascular Exercise

60 minutes of high-intensity cardio workouts like sprint intervals, circuit training, or kickboxing.

Abdominal Exercises

Incorporate advanced exercises such as hanging leg raises with twists, ab wheel rollouts, and plank variations with added weights.
Month 6

Strength Training

Exercise Sets Reps
Weighted Plank with Alternating Arm and Leg Lifts 3 Hold for 60 seconds
Jackknife Sit-ups 3 12 reps
Dragon Flags 3 10 reps
Weighted Cable Crunches 3 10 reps

Cardiovascular Exercise

60-75 minutes of mixed cardio workouts combining both high-intensity and steady-state exercises for maximum calorie burn.

Abdominal Exercises

Continue with challenging exercises like dragon flags, hanging leg raises with twists, and plank variations with added weights and stability challenges.
